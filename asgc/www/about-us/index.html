About Us

At CortexIMS, we understand the challenges faced by the construction industry when it comes to digital transformation. With years of experience, our team has developed a comprehensive software solution that encompasses every aspect of construction management.

  • Mission: To digitalize the globe that you are dreaming to build, to incorporate operation, quality, and EHS considerations into all human endeavours in order to promote prosperity and improve people's well-being.

  • Vision: Vision: To go nowhere anyone has gone before. First footprint everywhere! Humanity of our crew is a priority.

  • At CortexIMS our values guide everything we do. They shape our culture, drive our decisions, and define our future:

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all our endeavors.

  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and continuously seek new ways to improve and innovate, staying ahead in an ever-evolving landscape.

  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of our work, delivering outstanding results and exceeding expectations.

  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration, working closely with clients, partners, and colleagues to achieve shared goals.

  • Diversity and Inclusion: We celebrate diversity and foster an inclusive environment where every voice is heard, valued, and respected.

  • Customer Focus: We are dedicated to understanding and exceeding our customers' needs, delivering exceptional value and service.

  • Sustainability: We are committed to making a positive impact on society and the environment, integrating sustainability into everything we do.

  • These values define who we are and guide us as we strive to make a difference in the world.

The organization cooperates with a number of regional and international institutions concerned with human dignity and a better life.
